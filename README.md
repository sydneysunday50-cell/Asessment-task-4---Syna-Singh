<h2 > Assessment 2: Completed Action</h2 >

<h3 > Getting a Glass of Filtered Water</h3 >

<p >
In this completed action, I asked my friend for consent before recording her getting a glass of water.
I ensured there was no discomfort involved. The lighting was intentionally dimmed to improve focus on movement.
</p >

<p >
The goal was to explore screen media fundamentals using a simple action. I focused on frame, lighting, and ethical awareness.
</p >

<p >
During the process, I changed from bright lighting to dim lighting, which created a more focused and intimate atmosphere.
</p >

<p >
The final result showed the rhythm of the action, but lighting reduced visibility in some frames. I could improve by using better angles and clearer lighting.
</p >

<p >
This task taught me that even simple actions can tell a story without dialogue. Next time, I would experiment more with lighting, angles, and sound.
</p >
